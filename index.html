About

The Left Leg Kickers are a four-piece original rock band from Lanarkshire, Scotland, bringing loud guitars, sharp hooks and high-energy live shows with a country edge. Built on riffs, chemistry and a crowd-first attitude, they’re here to make noise and mean it.
