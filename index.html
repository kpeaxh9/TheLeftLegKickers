About

The Left Leg Kickers are a four-piece original rock band from Lanarkshire, Scotland. Mixing electric guitar, acoustic warmth, and solid rhythms and bass, into feel-good songs you’ll want to sing along to. Formed in late November 2024, we are all about fun, good vibes, and music that brings people together.
